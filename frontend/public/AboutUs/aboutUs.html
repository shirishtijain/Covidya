CoVidya

An Initiative to Optimise Digital Healthcare Infrastructure

We are more than a website.

Visualise Data
Due to COVID-19 public health emergency , there has been a steady increase in the demand for hospital beds , stretchers , and mattresses to expand health care capacity in local hospitals and health care systems.
Keeping this increased demand in mind , it would be a boon to general public they can get all the info related to covid just by a click.As our site provides all the relevant information regarding beds, oxygen cylinders, essential medicines , vaccination data etc it would be a boon for the users.

Our motives.

We strive to make the best use of web services in order to improve the quality of life and healthcare services..

Problems Solved

Considering the multifaceted threats posed by the pandemic, experiences on a close and personal scale as well, ignominy in efficiency is inexcusable. With this in mind, we keep efficiency at the top of our design paradigm. Our website would solve various problems with respect to organisation of resources and data, as well as allow community interaction for maintenance of an interactive discussion based structure which would facilitate community engagement.

Data Management

Using interactive graphs and real time data, we would map out the different facts and figures of the pandemic, classified by criterias and state, district etc. This visual representation would allow decision makers as well as common people to gauge the situation.

Resources Optimisation

We would include a resource dashboard with user referrals, feedback and maintenance. This would cover different essential resources which are needed like hospital beds data, oxygen data, and essential medicines. In order to run these features, we would have a volunteer form as well as a login page.
